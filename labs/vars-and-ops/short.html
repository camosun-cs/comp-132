Task 2: Math with Money

Write a Java program that splits a given amount of money into smaller monetary units. The program should let the user enter an amount of money as a single decimal number, and output a report listing the monetary equivalent in dollars, quarters, dimes, nickels, and pennies, using the largest denominations possible.

Your approach should be something like:

  1. Prompt the user to enter an amount of money (eg. 11.72)
  2. Convert the amount into cents (eg. 1156)
  3. Divide by 100 to find the dollar amount (eg. 11)
  4. Divide the remainder by 25 to find the number of quarters (eg. 2)
  5. Divide the remainder of that by 10 for dimes (eg. 1)
  6. Divide the remainder of that by 5 for nickels (eg. 1)
  7. The final remainder is the number of pennies (eg. 2)
